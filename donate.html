Partnership

Stand with the work of formation.

The hidden work of discipleship is sustained not by spectacle but by steady partnership. Your giving makes this work possible — in lives, in communities, and across generations.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 Corinthians 9:7
Where Your Gift Goes

Every partnership bears fruit.

Your support funds four interconnected streams of formation — each essential to the calling of The Ezra Centre.

i

Teaching Programs

Retreats, workshops, and ongoing discipleship gatherings that equip saints to walk the incarnational path.

ii

Publishing & Resources

Books, study guides, and written reflections that extend the ministry beyond the reach of any single gathering.

iii

Leadership Formation

Mentoring pastors, elders, and emerging leaders in the long, slow work of being formed before they form others.

iv

Digital Outreach

Carrying the ministry of the Word to saints across the nations through teaching content, podcasts, and online community.

How We Gather

Three kinds of formation.

Each program is designed to serve a distinct season of the disciple's journey — the first exposure, the deeper walk, the lifelong cohort.

i.

Teaching Intensives

Single-day or weekend gatherings open to all. The doorway into our ministry — concentrated teaching on the foundational themes of formation.

ii.

Annual Retreats

Residential gatherings set apart from ordinary life. Three to five days of worship, teaching, rest, and honest fellowship — typically by registration.

iii.

Leadership Cohorts

Extended fellowships for pastors and emerging leaders. Small groups meeting monthly over six to twelve months — by application only.

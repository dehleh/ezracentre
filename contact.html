Frequently Asked

Questions we often hear.

If your question is not answered here, please write — we would much rather hear from you directly.

Is The Ezra Centre a church?
No. The Ezra Centre is a teaching and formation ministry that exists alongside the local church, not in place of it. We equip saints and serve leaders — we do not gather as a congregation for Sunday worship. We strongly encourage every participant in our programs to be rooted in a faithful local church.
How can I become a partner?
You can partner through regular monthly giving at any level — Friend, Companion, or Patron — or through one-time gifts, bank transfers, legacy giving, or in-kind contributions. Visit our Partner page for the full details, or simply write to us.
Are your programs open to everyone?
Our teaching intensives and online seminars are open to all. The annual retreat is by registration with limited capacity. Our leadership cohorts are by application only, as they require sustained, close fellowship over many months.
Do you offer bulk book orders for churches or schools?
Yes — we offer meaningful discounts for churches, seminaries, study groups, and cohorts. Select "Book order / bulk pricing" in the contact form above and we will send you a tailored quote.
Can the founders speak at our event or conference?
The founders accept a limited number of external speaking invitations each year, as the integrity of the ministry requires that teaching flow from a settled life rather than a travel schedule. Please write with full details of your event and we will respond prayerfully.
How do I submit a prayer request?
Use the form above and select "Prayer request" from the subject dropdown. Your request will be treated in strict confidence and brought before the Lord. We do not share requests publicly unless you specifically ask us to.
